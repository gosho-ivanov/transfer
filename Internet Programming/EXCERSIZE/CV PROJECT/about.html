About Me

My name is Georgi Ivanov, I am 18 years old and deeply interested in the world of back-end development and embedded systems. My journey with programming began with curiosity about how machines work, and it grew into a passion for building efficient, low-level solutions that connect software and hardware.

I enjoy working on embedded projects, databases, and system logic. While I have tried front-end development, I find my strongest skills are in the technical and problem-solving aspects of back-end development.

In my free time, I love experimenting with electronics, exploring new programming concepts, and contributing to open-source projects on GitHub.
