AVENGED SEVENFOLD

Avenged Sevenfold is an American heavy metal band from California. The band is known for their diverse rock sound and dramatic imagery in album covers and merchandise.
They emerge with a metalcorte sound on their debut album Sounding the Seventh Trumpet and largely continued this sound through their second album Waking the Fallen.
Their styled had evolved, however, by the time the 3rd album "City of Evil, into a more traditional heav metal style. They have, to this day, continued to use this style, albeit with a lot of experimenting.
This is especially true with their latest, 8th album Life Is But A Dream..., a highly experimental record.
